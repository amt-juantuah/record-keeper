Note:

  1. You can save all your daily transactions safely on famBoard
  2. You can also delete a record you no longer need
  3. You are required to choose whether the record or tansaction generates direct income or expense. If your record is not associated with either your incomes or expense, then go ahead and select any, and leave the amount section as empty
  4. You are required to give a description of the record. Eg. Sold 10 bags of yam tubers; Or, Paid my debt owed to Nafisa; Or Payed workers on the farm
  5. It is optional to identify the date of the record or transaction. If left empty, current day's date will be picked
  6. You can also set the name of the person or organisation you engaged or had the transaction with. Eg. If you payed money or recieved money from someone, you can write the person's name
  7. If the record does not involve an amount payed or recieved, leave as empty or enter zero(0).
